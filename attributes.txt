// ATTRIBUTE DESCRIPTIONS
Name – The character's name.
Class – Defines the character's type.
Health – The number of health points (HP).
Stamina – The amount of stamina points for physical abilities.
Attack – The attack value, used to calculate physical damage.
Defense – The defense value, used to reduce incoming damage.
Dexterity – Influences who attacks first and stealth.
